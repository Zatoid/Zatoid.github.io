# Dark Souls is a Wonderful Game

Dark souls is something of a fever dream. I remember as a kid playing the very first souls game, Demon's Souls. I was immediately captivated by the dreary atmosphere. I wanted nothing more than to see what was hiding around the corner. My first encounter with Patches was a memorable moment, one which sealed in the idea that he is a real piece of work. I loved Demon's Souls and to this day it still holds a very special place in my heart.

Then Dark souls happened.

The very first time I laid hands on Dark Souls, I was immediately uncertain. Demon's Souls, despite it's janky nature, held your hand for the most part. The level design and sense of progression was fairly linear. All you had to do was simply walk forward and brute force your way to the end. Dark Souls was anything but hand holdy. The first time you set foot in Lordran, your not just granted options. You are given full on paths to follow. Some of those paths lead into one another, while others took you on the journey of a lifetime.
